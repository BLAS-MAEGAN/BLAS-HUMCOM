Book Title: Over And Over  (A JenLisa AU)
Book Type: Fan Fiction
Written By: Kim-Chichu
Date Published: 12.04.2018


"Over And Over"  is a girlxgirl story published in Wattpad and is currently my favorite book. It is a fan fiction made by the great author behind the name Kim-Chichu.

The story is about Lisa, a foreign exchange student from Thailand, finds her world, everything she knows and believes tested to their limits, along with her sanity when one day she enters a hidden room on her campus and walks into an American high school in 1964. and met an undeaniably gorgeous korean girl called Jennie Kim, who makes her life even more difficult and thrilling.

The book is a mixed type of mystery and fantasy as it contains time travelling, unravelling of secrets and unsolved cases way back in 1964. I can say that this book is by farthest, the best fan fiction I've ever read. It is a masterpiece which is, enough to put me into a roller coaster ride and had me feeling mixed emotions because of how the story detailed the story was, and stably written.
