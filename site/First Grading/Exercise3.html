Mac n Cheese Recipe


Ingredients

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Butter
  • Hotdog
  • Mushroom, sliced
  • 1 kl Spaghetti Sauce
  • Condensed milk (Optional)
  • Cheese
  • Nestle Cream
  • Ground meat
  • Garlic

Equipments:

  • Frying Pan
  • Caserole
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Wooden Spoon
  • Saucepan
  • Serving Dish

Procedure:

  1. Fry the minced garlic using the butter in the pan.
  2. Cook the macaroni pasta according to the packet instructions.
  3. When the garlic is already brown, put the sliced hotdog in the pan.
  4. Get another big caserole and cook the spaghetti sauce on it according to your taste, make sure to mix in the fried ingredients from a while ago.
  5. While stirring, mix in the mushroom to the sauce.
  6. Drain the cooked macaroni pasta, put it in the mixing bowl and immediately pour in the cooked sauce.
  7. Dish up onto container.
  • For the cheese, set aside, put the remaining butter on the pan.
  • Add the nestle cream depending on your choice. The more the merrier.
  • Put on the grated cheese while stirring.
  • Stirr until the cheese melts.
  • Season it, add up some condensed milk for the taste.
  • When done, put it on top of the macaroni and there you have you Mac n Cheese.
